Biography

Ferhat Ozgur Catak is an AI and Cyber Security Researcher of Information Security and Communication Technology Department at NTNU - Norwegian University of Science and Technology. His research interests include machine learning, cyber security, malware analysis and deep learning.

Interests

  • Cyber Security
  • Malware Analysis
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine/Deep Learning Methods for Cyber-Security
  • Distributed machine learning
  • Homomorphic encryption based Privacy-preserving machine learning
