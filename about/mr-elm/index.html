About

2020-05-25 4 min read

Ferhat Ozgur Catak is an artificial intelligence & security researcher at NTNU Norwegian University of Science and Technology. He has a Ph.D. degree from Istanbul University, graduate school of science, department of Informatics. His research interests focus on Cyber Security, Malware Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Machine/Deep Learning Methods for Cyber-Security, Distributed machine learning, Homomorphic encryption based Privacy-preserving machine learning. Dr. Catak has made contributions to distributed machine learning algorithms, feature selection on large scale datasets based on evolutionary algorithms, Fuzzy AHP based decision making. His works are published in SCI-indexed journals and international conferences. Dr. Catak is also a reviewer, editor at SCI-indexed international journals, invited speaker in the international conferences, the referee in grant projects.
